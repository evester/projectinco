FAQ's

 How was InCo created?  

InCo is the result of a group project under the aid of the program IB CAS. The CAS has given us the opportunity to mobilise in the field of volunteer work and therefore to reach out to the various institutions which we work with.

What is InCo’s aim?  

Our goal is to promote volunteering as much as possible among the students of Greek schools. We want to facilitate students' access to volunteering and, as we are aware of the great demand of Greek institutions for resources and human resources, we thought of creating the InCo website

What are some difficulties we faced in the process? 

While trying to accomplish this goal, we faced some difficulties in the effort and cooperation of the members. As we expanded our work to other schools beyond our own, we also encountered problems with the schools and the institutions. 

What did we gain from this experience? 

We have acquired important knowledge such as cooperation, solidarity and the ability to listen to others' problems. The InCo experience has been insightful as we have come in contact with new people seeking our help and institutions that aim to contribute to the Greek community.
